Meet Our Legal Team

Our legal team brings together extensive expertise in Maritime (Admiralty) Law, International Trade, and Customs, offering comprehensive legal and commercial consultancy services to clients across the shipping and trade industries. With years of practice before Admiralty Courts, Commercial Courts, High Courts, and Customs Courts, we are well-positioned to handle complex disputes and provide practical, result-oriented solutions.

We specialize in a wide spectrum of maritime and trade-related matters, including ship arrest, P&I Club settlement and ship release, maritime insurance claims, ship registration, and renewal ,company registration proceedings. Our services also extend to documentation and customs clearance, freight forwarding, ship chandelling, crew changing, and recruitment/placement under RPS licensing. Through our integrated legal and commercial approach, we ensure that shipowners, traders, and logistics providers receive seamless support at every stage of their operations.

Beyond litigation and consultancy, we are actively engaged in awareness programs and training sessions covering key topics such as maritime arbitration, international trade practices, hydrography, and port-related governance. These initiatives reflect our commitment to building knowledge and resilience among stakeholders in the maritime and logistics sectors.

With a balance of professional practice, academic contribution, and industry engagement, our team provides trusted guidance for shipowners, operators, exporters, importers, and all those navigating the complexities of maritime law and international commerce.
