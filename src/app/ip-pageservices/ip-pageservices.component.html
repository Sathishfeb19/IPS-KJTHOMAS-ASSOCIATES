Our Expertise

With more than 10 years of practice, our firm specializes in a wide range of legal issues, offering comprehensive services across various jurisdictions and dispute resolution platforms. Our expertise is focused on the following key areas:

Maritime & Admiralty Law

  • Admiralty Jurisdiction: Handling legal matters related to ships, cargo, and maritime disputes.
  • Charterparty Issues: Advising and representing clients on charterparty agreements.
  • P&I Club Settlements: Facilitating settlements with Protection and Indemnity Clubs.
  • Marine Insurance: Navigating complex marine insurance claims.
  • Port-related Issues: Addressing legal challenges that arise within port operations.
  • Marine Pollution: Providing legal counsel on environmental and pollution-related maritime incidents.
  • Salvage Issues: Assisting with salvage claims and related legal processes.

International Trade & Customs Law

  • Exim Trade Providing legal guidance on import and export trade regulations.
  • Customs : Representing clients in Customs Appeals and before tribunals such as CESTAT and CERAT

Dispute Resolution

  • Litigation Representing clients in various courts, including Commercial Courts, High Courts, and Civil and Criminal Courts
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) : Handling matters through International Arbitration and Mediation.
